FAQ

Is ShareText free to use?

Yes. This demo version is fully free and designed for quick sharing and prototyping.

Does this store my text on a server?

No server storage is used in this front-end demo. Your text stays in your browser session.

Can I share long messages?

Yes, but very long text can produce long URLs. Use copy-to-clipboard for best reliability.

Can I customize branding and colors?

Absolutely. The styles are modular so you can quickly adapt the theme for your brand.
